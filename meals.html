Wang's Dim Sum

Wang's Dim Sum is a family owned Dim Sum restaurant founded in 1987 in Beverly Hills, California. Chef and owner Jim Wang has been cooking since he was a young boy growing up in West Covina, California. Wang and his restaurant have been hit hard by COVID-19, and the reduction of customers means he might need to close down due to the high rent in Beverly Hills. Support him by ordering food through us!
